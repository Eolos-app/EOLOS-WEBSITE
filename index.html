The Paradox

⚡ The Energy Paradox

Every watt consumed by traditional purifiers represents fossil fuels burned, emissions released, and environmental cost extracted. EOLOS operates at zero watts, breaking this destructive cycle permanently.

🗑️ The Waste Problem

Standard filters become landfill within months. Our biochar core transforms into garden nutrients, completing a circular journey from earth to home and back to earth — zero disposal, only regeneration.

🔇 The Silence Imperative

Clean air shouldn't demand noise pollution. Natural convection operates at 0 decibels — your purification is felt, not heard, restoring peace to your environment alongside cleaner air.

Three Pillars of Innovation

Physics, chemistry,
and circularity

01

Thermodynamic Induction

The precision-engineered cylindrical chassis creates a natural chimney effect. As indoor temperatures fluctuate throughout the day, air is drawn upward through filtration by temperature differentials alone — no fans, no motors, no moving parts.

50–150m³/hr airflow rate
02

Molecular Carbon Sequestration

Our proprietary Biochar-Active core achieves over 1,000 m² of surface area per gram — equivalent to 2.5 football pitches in every teaspoon. VOCs, formaldehyde, and CO₂ are captured through van der Waals forces and chemical bonding.

99.7%VOC capture rate
03

Nutrient-Cycle Circularity

After 18–24 months, your filter doesn't become landfill — it becomes garden nourishment. The saturated biochar core breaks down into bio-available nutrients that feed plant growth, completing a full-circle journey from atmosphere to earth.

18–24months effective lifespan
