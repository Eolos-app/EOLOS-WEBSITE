The Biochar Core

Our proprietary filter doesn't just trap pollutants; it locks carbon in a stable form. After 24 months, it becomes nutrient-rich soil for your plants.

Circularity

From Air to Soil.

01

Capture

Harmful VOCs, CO2, and odors are sequestered within the microscopic pores of the Biochar core via molecular attraction.

02

Transformation

Once saturated (18-24 months), the core is removed. No plastic waste, no toxic filters, no landfills.

03

Regeneration

The core is crushed and mixed with garden soil. The captured carbon then supports microbial life and plant growth.
